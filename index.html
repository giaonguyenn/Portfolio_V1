About Me

As a full-stack web developer with a background in marketing and advertising, I am eager to absorb and incorporate all the latest frameworks and libraries to contribute to your company's professional goals. Qualifications include my technical skills, keen eye for details and receptive learning. Let's chat!

  • AUG 2009 - DEC 2012

    Student

    The University of Texas at Austin

    Becoming a Longhorn was one of the best decisions of my life. I joined various student organizations and a sorority where I gained most of my team-building and leadership skills. Austin was a great place for me to find and develop myself personally and professionally. Hook 'em horns!

  • AUG 2013 - APRIL 2015

    Managing Director of Sales and Business Development

    Ed Mehlman and Associates

    For my first big-girl job, I was put to the test as the lead of a small marketing team. We successfully developed marketing collateral and campaigns that revamped the way we reached out to potential clients. I developed content, created mock-ups and worked closely with a developer to refresh our company site. This was my first taste of the coding world.

  • FEB 2016 - JULY 2017

    Account / Brand Coordinator

    The LOOMIS Agency

    Fueling my desire, I took a deep dive into the chaotic world of advertising and loved it, for the most part. I was exposed to so many creative minds but still felt the need to find a position where I can develop my own content rather than work behind the scenes. I learned so much about the art of client relationship and am grateful for the connections I made but was finally ready to take an even bigger leap.

  • AUG 2017 - NOV 2017

    Student Web Developer

    DevMountain Web Development Immersive Program

    Starting my career over was a scary thought so I knew I needed structure and support. What better way to jump off into the abyss than through a bootcamp with 20+ languages, techs, tools to learn in 13 weeks? It was so intense, but so worth it. I tested my receptive learning to the max and had the privilege to work amongst some of the most brilliant people. With all that under my belt, I’m definitely ready to tackle the next chapter.
